About Me
Jack Demaree
I attended Versailles, Indiana Grade and High School. I was considered the class clown. I was voted the most likely to not succeed. I spent most of my high school time in the study hall. I Have been married to the same gal for 49 years. I have a very successful in home business creating web pages for local business. I have been a Ham Radio operator for over 45 years and have bounced radio signals off the moon. I have a few friends, both local and around the world. I have talked to over 300  ARRL  countries. I am a Vietnam era veteran, member of the American Radio Rely League (ARRL), I worked 27 years and retired from United Parcel Service. I think I've did all right, what do you think ?
