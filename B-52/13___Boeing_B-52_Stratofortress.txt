1/144 Lockheed D-21 - Foxone
1/144 Lockheed D-21 - Foxone
A high speed photo reconnaissance drone developed in the early 1960s, originally intended to be launched from the
M-21
which was a converted
A-12
(a
SR-71
kit will have to make do!), it was later configered to work from the
B-52
(which carried one under each wing on pylons). The programme was cancelled after a small number of flights over China!
This resing kit allows you to build one of two options!
http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lockheed_D-21
Available Now:
August 2011
Price: 2000 Yen
