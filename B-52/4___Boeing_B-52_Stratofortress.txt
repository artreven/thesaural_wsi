1966 Dancing On a Cold Wind
When I was coming out of sleep state at dawn, I saw Michael M Mauldin (South African who lived in the USA). He was happy and smiling at me, then I saw a gas mask from WW2, like a child would wear. Clearly, this was to do with a war.
After that I was given a vision of a Tibetan singing bowl, then it was filled with some of kind of white meal like porridge. Then I saw people in prisons getting ready to be released. There were a lot of people together.
I was shown the hemp field like the one in Norfolk, England. My mother was evacuated there when she was a child during WW2.
After that I was shown Vietnam, then the City of London, not far from where we lived during the time of the Vietnam war. I could hear the song ' She flew across the room',
from CARMEN's 'Dancing on a Cold Wind' album. Carmen, were a British-American flamenco rock band, three of the members were from the USA and the other two were from England. The album is now available on CD.
One of the band members also passed over. The base player John Glascock, died when he was just 28 years old in 1979. He had a heart defect like Michael. Both of them should have received proper medical treatment at the right time. If they had both received the right treatment at the right time, they would both be a alive today. So many people die due to neglect. Michael called for urgent help, and the help was not given to him when he called. He was dead for three weeks before they found him, that just shows you how much they cared. Hence, why he wrote all over the internet that he had no family.
In the song it mentions that she let out a scream. When I was in St Augustine, Florida, I woke up screaming, I had seen the bush fires coming closer and closer to where we were, and I was screaming 'They're not listening, they're not listening'. Michael reassured me that everything was OK, and he held me gently in his arms, as I came back into full consciousness.
Michael is mentioned in the book of Daniel and also in the Gospel of Thomas. He is written in the lambs book of life. He stood with me against Obama (Rev 13), and the Prince of Persia mentioned in the book of Daniel.
I was then given the message
'Non-violence, Sophisticated'.
Sophisticated was one of the words that Michael used to say to me.
He knows that it is a word that identifies him.
I asked when and where? I was told
'1966'.
In 1966, there was a gas leak in France. There was a conference on Rhodesia in Nigeria and a military coup followed deposing the civilian government. Then there was another military coup, and it began the long military rule. The UK ceased all trade with Rhodesia. Michael and his mother moved to the USA after the Rhodesian government were overthrown.
The war was still going on in Vietnam and on the 26th of March there were demonstrations across the USA in protest of it. 26 is the Hebrew gematria value of the name of the LORD God. 20,000 Buddhists marched in protest against the polices of the government in Southern Vietnam.
"A  B-52  bomber collides with a  KC-135 Stratotanker  over Spain, dropping three 70-kiloton  hydrogen bombs near the town of  Palomares , and one into the sea, in the  1966 Palomares B-52 crash ."
There was also flight 911 that crashed in Japan. Off the coast of Spain, a submarine also found another missing American H bomb. What were they doing dropping H bombs near Spain?
England won the FIFA world cup against West Germany.
