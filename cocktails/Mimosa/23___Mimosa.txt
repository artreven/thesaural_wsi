Mimosa Infesta
is a giant carnivorous plant that functions as the third boss of
Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles: Dark Horizons . It is accompanied by mutant mushrooms.
Its name comes from
Mimosa , a plant genus, and
infesta , a Latin, Spanish, Italian, and French word for infestation.
