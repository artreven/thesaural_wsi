Baruch College - The City University of New York
Baruch College , located in
Manhattan, New York,
is a public university and one of the colleges comprising the City University of New York (CUNY). The
Zicklin Shool of Business
is home to the
Stan Ross Department of Accountancy
that offers students both undergraduate and graduate accounting degrees. You can find information about the PhD accounting program
here
or general information about Baruch's graduate programs
here.
The accounting department was recently ranked by the Public Accounting Record as the 20th best undergraduate program, the 22nd best graduate program, and an honorable mention as a PhD program.
Living Conditions
Manhattan
has a climate that is characterized by warm, humid summers and snowy winters. The cost of living in New York City is 65% above the national average.
