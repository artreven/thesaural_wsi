Jumpstart is a
Mini-Con
from the
video game
version of the
Unicron Trilogy .
Don't ask where the jumper cables attach.
Jumpstart
is one of many power-enhancing Mini-Cons scattered across
Earth .
A member of the
Red Mini-Con Team , when
Powerlinxed
to a larger Transformer, Jumpstart transforms into a powerful electrical blaster. Enemies near the target struck by the beam may also take damage as the bolt leaps from enemy to enemy.

Fiction
Transformers Playstation 2 Game
Jumpstart's
Mini-Con storage panel
was discovered on
Megatron's
Pacific island
base, on a tiny ledge on the sheer rock face of the volcano, above the deep blue sea. It was only with the use of
Slipstream 's glider-pack that the Autobots were able to recover him. Following the defeat of Megatron, he and the other Mini-Cons returned to
Cybertron
and helped form the
Matrix Cannon ,
Nemesis Shield
and
Vanguard Booster , giving the Autobots the power needed to destroy
Unicron .
Notes
Jumpstart uses the same base model as
Aurora ,
Corona , and
Sparkjump .
The launch party for the
Armada
game had a "Mini-Con Madness" drink menu.
A "Jumpstart" was a
Cosmopolitan .
