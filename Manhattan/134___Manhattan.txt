Columbia University
Columbia University
is a private university located in
Manhattan, New York. Columbia's
Business School
is home to an
accounting division
that offers students PhD accounting degrees. You can find information about the PhD accounting program
here
or general information about Columbia's business graduate programs
here.
Living Conditions
Manhattan
has a climate that is characterized by warm, humid summers and snowy winters. The cost of living in New York City is 65% above the national average. However, Columbia's Accounting program is generous in its stipend which exceeds the stipend for other doctoral students at Columbia. Students are guaranteed RA-ships and TA-ships over and above their fellowships. Finally, students are provided with subsidized rental university housing, which is guaranteed for five years.
