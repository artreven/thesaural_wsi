Second Destination: IBIZA (Iberian peninsula, Spain)
Filed under:

—
Leave a comment
March 19, 2010
Indeed, another perfect destination for those who would just like to have fun…
Ibiza
( Catalan :  Eivissa ) is an island in the  Mediterranean Sea
79 km off the coast of the city of Valencia in  Iberian Peninsula ,  Spain
I biza is considered a popular tourist destination, especially due to its legendary and at times riotous nightlife centered around two areas:  Ibiza Town , the island’s capital on the southern shore and  Sant Antoni
to the West
Best Night Spot:
Privilege Ibiza
is the “world’s largest  nightclub ” according to the  Guinness Book of Records with a capacity of 10,000 persons. It is located not far from  San Rafael ,  Ibiza , less than a kilometer from the nightclub  Amnesia .
