Call of Duty: Black Ops Edit
Overview Edit
The M14 is a weapon which can be bought off the wall in most maps in
Call of Duty: Black Ops
and
Call of Duty: Black Ops II
for 500  points  in the first room. The M14 has an eight round magazine. Ammo can be bought off the wall for 250 points.
Kino der Toten Edit
in
Kino der Toten , the wallbuy location is at the top of the right hand staircase in the Starting Room.
"Five" Edit
In
" Five " , if the player looks towards the large television screen from the spawn, they will find the M14 in the back along the wall to their left.
Ascension Edit
The M14 wall buy is directly opposite the lander which the player(s) spawn from.
Call of the Dead Edit
The Wall buy is adjacent to the
Olympia
on the small bridge section
Shangri-La Edit
On the right side of the Spawn room next to a Gong
Moon Edit
After opening the first door, it is on the right side of the Recieving Bay.
Bus Depot/TranZit Edit
The Wallbuy is on the far side of the
Turbine
powered door.
Town Edit
Next to a window near the bank, where the bus would enter in TranZit.
Die Rise Edit
Next to the Quick Revive elevator behind the door.
Mob of the Dead Edit
End of the Hallway opposite the library.
Buried Edit
On a wooden plank near the Olympia wall buy
Origins
Edit
On the wall next to the shovel in the starting room.
Mnesia Edit
When placed in the
Pack-a-Punch Machine , it will be called the
Mnesia . After the upgrade, it has a 16 round magazine, 192 in reserve, increased damage, and a Grip is added. However, it's still not a good weapon to use on high rounds.

Trivia Edit
The Pack-a-Punched name, Mnesia, may be a play of the word,
Amnesia , a sickness that causes memory disturbance or loss.
When PaP'ed, it is the favored weapon of
Takeo Masaki . Incidentally, he was also the first person to break his amnesia.
