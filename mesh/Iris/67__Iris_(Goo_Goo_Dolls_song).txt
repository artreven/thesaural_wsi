The Voice Edit
Charlotte Sometimes
Charlotte Sometimes is a contestant on Season 2 of The Voice. For her audition she sang "Apologize" and was picked by every coach. She chose
Blake Shelton . In the battle rounds she was paired against Lex Land. They sang
Pumped Up Kicks
by
Foster the People . She won so Lex was eliminated.
In the first live rounds, She performed
Paramore's
Misery Business . She did not garner enough votes and performed in the last chance rounds. For the last chance round, she sang
Iris
by
the Goo Goo Dolls .
Coach Blake saved fellow contestant and teammate Jordis Unga instead therefore eliminating Charlotte.
